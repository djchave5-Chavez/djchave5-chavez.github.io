External resources

Here are some other common resources that you may find helpful

The PSU Tier List

The PSU tier list is a community-driven project that gives a quick reference guide for any given PSU's reliability and safety. Generally, new builds should be using a tier B or better, or tier C if you're on a really tight budget.

Tom's Hardware GPU comparison chart

The news site Tom's Hardware gives a visual breakdown of GPU performance by model in one graph. Simply pick the resolution you plan to build for and the chart will give you a good estimate on how any given card stacks up against its peers.

How to build a PC full tutorial

This is the most complete building guide I know of, and gives a much more detailed explanation than a static site could. While it may not cover your specific build, this video combined with reading the instruction manuals for your parts should be more than enough.
